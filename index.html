❤ Memere's Macaroni & Cheese ❤

By Arlene (Memere) Lanoie

Cook Time:
15 minutes
Serves:
3-4 people
  • What to Buy
  • How to Make it

Ingredients

Note about ingredients.

  • 8 ounces of elbow macaroni
  • 1½ cups of milk
  • 8 ounces of Velveeta cheese
  • Panko bread crumbs

Directions

something here

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°
  2. Boil a pot of water and add the 8 ounces of macaroni. Cook macaroni for desired amount of time, then drain.
  3. Cut the 8 ounces of Velveeta cheese into 1cm cubes.
  4. Add the cheese cubes and 1½ cups of milk to a small pot. Put mixture on the stove at medium heat, and stir continuously so the cheese does not stick.
  5. Put the macaroni in a casserole dish, and pour in the cheese mixture.
  6. Finally top with desired amount of bread crumbs. Leave in the oven for around 15 minutes or until bubbling.

About the Author

This macaroni & cheese recipe was passed down to my grandmother from Memere, and then passed down to my mom! It is such a great recipe because of how easy it is to make but it's still delicious!
